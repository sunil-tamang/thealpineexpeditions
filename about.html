WHO WE ARE ?


"A Himalayan Based Trekking Company that deals with the adventurous activities like trekking, camping ,white water rafting ,paragliding . Being born in the Himalayas we have the better experience and knowledge of mountains terrain than any other travel company .

Our passion is to introduce you to the true spirit of the Himalayas, be it through an intimate family journey, an educational trip for schools and colleges, or a bespoke adventure tailored just for you. Above all, we prioritize your safety. We believe that adventure should never come at the cost of well-being, and that's why we work only with certified guides who understand the profound value of life and the intricacies of these mountainous paths.

Come, explore the Himalayas with those who call it home."
