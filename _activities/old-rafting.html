 Overview

Ready to go up against the rushing flow of mighty BEAS RIVER, packed with white water, in the middle of mountains with natural beauty! So pack your backpack and join us to capture your best ever River rafting experience.

The rivers originating from the Himalayas are considered the best match for rafting. From the narrow rivers, rafting is an activity of immense fun and thrill. The "narrow" part of the river increases the flow of water, which is very thrilling and gives an adrenaline rush for the people sitting on the raft and challenging for the raft guide. Let our experience and professionalism, not only guide you safely down to the river but also allow us to share with you, some of the wonderful activities and environmental phenomenon for your lifetime experience.
