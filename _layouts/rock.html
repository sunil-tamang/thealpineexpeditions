 Frequently Asked Questions

Our rock climbing trips are designed for climbers of all experience levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced climber, our guides will work with you to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

We provide all necessary climbing equipment, including harnesses, ropes, carabiners, and climbing shoes. You're welcome to bring your own equipment if you prefer.

A typical climbing day begins early in the morning with a hearty breakfast. We'll then head to the climbing location, where our guides will provide instruction and guidance throughout the day. We'll take breaks for lunch and snacks, and return to camp in the evening for dinner and relaxation.

Don't worry! Our guides are trained to work with climbers of all comfort levels. We'll work with you to build your confidence and provide support throughout the trip.

Safety is our top priority. Our guides are certified in wilderness first aid and CPR, and we carry a first aid kit with us at all times. We'll also provide instruction on climbing safety and best practices.
