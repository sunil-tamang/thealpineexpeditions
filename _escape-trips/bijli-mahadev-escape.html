Weekend Escape




 Overview

The WEEKEND ESCAPE TRIP is a short trip for the weekend. This trip is meant for escaping from the busy routine of work and to release the pressure of the whole week. Enjoy it while you can, and be on this trip, create new "Trippy" memories and be refreshed again.

 Escape Journey

  • DAY 0 - Meet at Manali

    Arrive in Manali & stay at Hotel or Guest House and preparation for next day trek

  • DAY 1 - RAISON - Matikochar - BIJLI MAHADEV

    Early morning drive 25 km from manali to raison and then a link road to Matikochar. From Matikochar, we start our trek to Bijli Mahadev through the Deodar Forest. It takes around 4 hours to reach at the Top. After reaching Bijli Mahadev, we will, after some rest, pitch our camp & collect some water & woods to cook food for dinner. At night, the whole group gets together around the bonfire, enjoying each other's company, while talking and having dinner.

  • DAY 2 - Bijli Mahadev - Puid Village TREK | Puid Village to Raison Drive

    Second day starts with morning breakfast and warm coffee or tea. Today we have a whole day to explore the nearby spots around our camp. There's a temple of Lord Shiva through which the place got its name, "BIJLI MAHADEV". We can explore that and other really beautiful landscapes from up above there. The whole Parvati valley and the Parvati river can be seen from the top. In the mid-day, we'll leave the Campsite to return from the trek.

  • DAY 3 - Rafting

    The third day is the day of Rafting and having fun at the riverside. We'll leave the Hotel/Guest House after breakfast for another recreational adventure popularly known as the White Water Rafting. First we'll enjoy at the riverside, following the guidelines and rules instructed by the Guide and then we'll go for the adventure of a lifetime - Rafting. In the Evening, you can take the bus from Manali Bus Stand.

 Things To Pack

  Trekking Shoes: You need good trekking shoes with good grip & ankle support. Do not carry sport shoes.

  Clothing: You need to have warm clothes with three-layer jackets, fleece or down feather jacket. Carry 1 or 2 full sleeves t-shirt. For lowers carry cotton pants suitable for cold weather.

  Thermals: Carry top and bottom thermals.

  Socks: Carry 2 - 3 pair of sports socks along with woolen socks to be worn at night.

  Headlamp/Led Torch are mandatory.

  Sunglasses: To protect from sunlight in snow you require UV rays protected sunglasses.

  Woolen Cap and Gloves are also necessary as the climate will be cold. Carry waterproof gloves as they get wet in snow.

  Lunchbox & Water bottle: Every participant should carry lunch box and water bottle of their own.

  Raincoat/Ponchos: At high altitudes, snowfall and rain are quite common and it’s mandatory to carry a poncho so that one doesn’t get wet.
